FAQ

Common questions.

What types of projects do you take on?
I specialise in Full-Stack web development (React + Node.js), Python scripting and automation, REST API design, and numerical/engineering computation (MATLAB/Python). I am happy to discuss any project in those domains — regardless of size.
Are you available before May 2026?
My full freelance launch is planned for May 2026 to coincide with completing my current semester. However, I can accommodate small projects or consultations before that date — please reach out and let me know your timeline.
What is your typical project workflow?
My standard workflow: (1) Brief — understanding your goals and constraints. (2) Proposal — scope, timeline and cost estimate. (3) Development — iterative with regular updates. (4) Review — your feedback integrated before final delivery. (5) Handoff — clean code, documentation and optional post-launch support.
Do you work with clients outside Djibouti?
Absolutely — I work fully remote and communicate in English, French and Arabic. My time zone (EAT, UTC+3) overlaps well with Europe, the Middle East, and East Africa. I use standard platforms (Upwork, Fiverr, GitHub, Notion) to keep collaboration smooth regardless of location.
How is payment handled?
For Upwork and Fiverr projects, payment is secured through the platform's escrow system — safe for both parties. For direct contracts, I require a 50% deposit before work begins, with the remainder due upon delivery. I accept PayPal, Wise and platform credits.
